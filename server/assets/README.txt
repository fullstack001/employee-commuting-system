Optional QR center logo
-----------------------
Place a PNG (or JPG via rename) here as:

  logo.png

Square logos work best. Recommended: at least 128×128 px, transparent background.
The image is scaled to ~20% of the QR size and drawn in the center.

Alternatively set QR_LOGO_PATH in .env to any absolute path on the server.

If no logo is present, the QR is generated without a center image (still includes the text footer).
